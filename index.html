Private by design

Wearable micro‑coaching that helps people regulate stress in the moment

NeuroEase uses on‑device signals to suggest short, evidence‑based actions, for example paced breathing or a one‑minute reframing prompt. Data stays local when possible, teams can opt into aggregated insights.

Prototype in testing Partner pilots, 2025 EU data residency

Mission

Build a practical assistant that notices early stress patterns and nudges helpful actions without getting in the way. Initial focus is workplace wellbeing, with clear value for individuals and organizations.

Product snapshot

Signal sensing

Heart rate dynamics and movement trends, processed locally where possible, tuned for short on‑wrist sessions.

Micro‑coaching

Brief cues and scripts that fit into a minute, based on acceptance and attention training, configurable to context.

Privacy controls

Clear boundaries, opt‑in sharing, and organization‑level summaries that avoid exposing personal details.
